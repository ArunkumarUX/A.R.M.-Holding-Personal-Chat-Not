       APPLICATION OF ENGLISH LAW REGULATIONS (AMENDMENT NO. 1) 2022

            Regulations amending the Application of English Law Regulations 2015

                                                             Date of Enactment: 11 March 2022

The Board of Directors of the Abu Dhabi Global Market, in exercise of its powers under Article
6(1) of Law No. 4 of 2013 (as amended) concerning the Abu Dhabi Global Market issued by His
Highness the Ruler of the Emirate of Abu Dhabi, hereby enacts the following Regulations:

1.     Amendments to the Application of English Law Regulations 2015

The Application of English Law Regulations 2015 are amended as follows:

(1)    In the ‘Schedule: Applicable Statutes’, paragraph (5) in the row concerning the ‘Bankers’
       Books Evidence Act 1879 (Chapter 11)’, will be deleted in its entirety and replaced in full
       by the following:

       “In section 11, the words “Sunday, Christmas Day, Good Friday, and any bank holiday”
       shall be substituted by the words “Saturday, Sunday, and any other day declared a public
       holiday in the United Arab Emirates””

(2)    In the ‘Schedule: Applicable Statutes’, paragraph (4) in the row concerning ‘Bills of
       Exchange Act 1882 (Chapter 61) as amended by the Small Business, Enterprise and
       Employment Act 2015’, the words “Friday or Saturday” will be deleted in their entirety and
       replaced in full by the following:

       “Saturday or Sunday”

(3)    In the ‘Schedule: Applicable Statutes’, paragraph (8) in the row concerning ‘Bills of
       Exchange Act 1882 (Chapter 61) as amended by the Small Business, Enterprise and
       Employment Act 2015’, the paragraph starting with “In section 92” shall be numbered
       paragraph (9) and the words “Friday, Saturday” will be deleted in their entirety and
       replaced in full by the following:

       “Saturday, Sunday”

       The numbering of the subsequent paragraphs in the row concerning ‘Bills of Exchange
       Act 1882 (Chapter 61) as amended by the Small Business, Enterprise and Employment
       Act 2015’ shall be updated accordingly.

2.     Short title, extent and commencement

(1)    These Regulations may be cited as the Application of English Law Regulations
       (Amendment No.1) 2022.
(2)    These Regulations shall apply in the Abu Dhabi Global Market.
(3)    These Regulations come into force on the date of their publication.
